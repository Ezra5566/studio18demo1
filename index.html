About

Where Fashion
Meets Culture

STUDIO 18 is more than a fashion brand—it's a cultural movement. Born from the vibrant streets of Nairobi and Kigali, we curate experiences that bridge the gap between contemporary streetwear and authentic African expression.

Through our pop-up events, collaborative collections, and immersive experiences, we're building a global community that celebrates creativity, individuality, and the power of cultural exchange.
